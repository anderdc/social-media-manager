a year ago I almost quit building in public. no engagement, no followers, just me posting into the void every single morning wondering if any of it mattered or if I was just shouting into an empty room while everyone else seemed to already have it figured out.

here's what nobody tells you: the void is where the work happens. those quiet months are not wasted, they are compounding. every post is a rep, every rep sharpens the next one, and one day you look up and the room is full and you have no idea when people started showing up because you were too busy doing the work to notice.

so if you are early and it feels lonely, good. that is the part most people quit before. stay in the room. 🌱
